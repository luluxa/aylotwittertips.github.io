6 ways to get more alpha from Crypto Twitter

  • Use Twitter Lists: organise the lists by category so you can quickly scan different sectors.

  • Use Tweet Deck: it's essential to organise your content.

  • Put key individuals and accounts on notifications. One example is @peckshield Be careful of who you choose (avoid influencers)

  • Use IFTTT. A incredible tool that allows you to automate actions across apps, devices and services. For example, you can save tweets with specific content in a google spreadsheet.

  • Monitor @chad_follows. He tweets who the big accounts are following in real time. It's usable to find interesting people to follow.

  • Bookmark tweets for you to read later. If it's available in your country, sign-up for Twitter Blue so you can organise into bookmark folders
